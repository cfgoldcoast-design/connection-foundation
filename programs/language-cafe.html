How it works

Simple, warm, and yours to join

01

Read together

Each session we read a short passage aloud as a group. Any level can follow along — the text is always accessible and thought-provoking.

02

Discuss freely

We share what resonated, what surprised us, what we disagree with. In English — but without pressure. There are no wrong answers, no corrections mid-sentence.

03

Connect & grow

Every week you leave with better English and stronger friendships. Many members also join community service activities together.
