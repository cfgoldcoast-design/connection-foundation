# Connection Foundation Gold Coast
## Visual Identity — v1 · May 2026

Community-led non-profit in Mermaid Waters (Gold Coast, Australia) running free programmes for personal and community transformation. Open to everyone, regardless of background or belief. Spiritually open, not religious.

**Voice.** Warm, direct, inclusive — a trusted friend, not a programme manager. No jargon, no pitch, no fee.

---

## 1. Logo

The mark is a constructivist geometric composition: a large wine circle, a rose arch enclosing a cream-and-wine donut, a small blush dot floating between, an ink square containing an ember circle, and three stone vertical bars.

**Files**

| File | Use |
|---|---|
| `logo-cfgc-transparent.png` | Primary mark, transparent background. Use on any palette surface (cream, wine, mulberry, blush). |
| `logo-cfgc.png` *(original, cream paper)* | When the logo needs its own "card" treatment on photography or busy backgrounds. |

**Rules**

- Do not redraw, recolour, rotate, skew or apply effects to the mark. Use the PNG as supplied.
- Keep a clear-space around the mark equal to the height of the cream-and-wine donut.
- Minimum size: 24 px tall on screen, 12 mm tall in print.
- Do not place the mark on photography without the cream card behind it.

## 2. Palette — Rosewood (locked)

Pure black and pure white are avoided throughout. Use `Cream` as the default surface, `Wine` as the default primary, `Ember` for calls-to-action.

| Role | Name | Hex | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Wine | `#5B1F3A` | Headlines, links, brand bars |
| Deep accent | Mulberry | `#8B2C4F` | Section dividers, poster surfaces |
| Secondary | Rose Dust | `#C97B8E` | Editorial accents, decorative shapes |
| Soft tint | Blush | `#E8B5BD` | Backgrounds for emphasis, cards |
| CTA | Ember | `#D45D5D` | Buttons, links-of-action, alerts |
| Background | Cream | `#F7F0E8` | Default page background |
| Body text | Ink | `#2A1A22` | All body copy; near-black, never pure black |
| Muted | Stone | `#8A7A7E` | Captions, meta, secondary info |

**Pairings**

- Cream + Wine + Ember → default web surface
- Mulberry + Blush + Cream → poster / hero surfaces
- Wine + Cream + Rose Dust → social posts

## 3. Typography — Helvetica only

One family, three weights. The system relies on **weight and scale contrast**, not on stylistic flourishes.

| Style | Size | Weight | Tracking | Line-height |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| H1 Display | 80 px | 500 | -3.5% | 0.98 |
| H2 Title | 48 px | 500 | -2.5% | 1.05 |
| H3 Heading | 28 px | 500 | -1.5% | 1.15 |
| Body | 17 px | 400 | 0 | 1.55 |
| Small | 14 px | 400 | 0 | 1.5 |
| Caption | 11 px | 500 | +12% UPPER | 1.4 |

Stack: `"Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`.

## 4. Tone of voice

- Talk to one person, not an audience.
- Short sentences. Active verbs. No marketing adjectives.
- It's "we", not "our organisation". It's "you", not "users".
- State facts directly: free, no booking, every Wednesday.
- Replace "transformation programme" with "a conversation", "a circle", "a class".

**Examples**

- ✅ "Come as you are. Leave with something."
- ✅ "There is no fee, no required reading, and no pitch."
- ❌ "Unlock your potential with our transformational journey."

---

## 5. Web and print rules

- Default page background: Cream `#F7F0E8`.
- Body text: Ink `#2A1A22`. Never black.
- Buttons: Ember pill, white text, no border, no shadow.
- Ghost buttons: 1 px Ink border, no fill.
- Links: Wine, no underline; underline on hover.
- Cards: 1 px Ink-12% border, no shadow.
- Section spacing: 120 px desktop, 64 px mobile.
