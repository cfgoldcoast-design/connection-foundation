How it works

Simple, warm,
and genuinely different

No textbooks, no tests, no awkward silence. Just real people having real conversations.

Real sessions, real people
01

Read together

Each session we read a short passage aloud as a group. Any level can follow along — the text is always accessible and thought-provoking.

02

Discuss freely

We share what resonated, what surprised us, what we disagree with. In English — but without pressure. There are no wrong answers, no corrections mid-sentence.

03

Connect & grow

Every week you leave with better English and stronger friendships. Many members also join community service activities together.

Why Connection Foundation

Not just another
language group

Gold Coast has plenty of English groups. Here is what makes this one different.

Every voice matters
🛡️

Zero judgment

No corrections mid-sentence. No embarrassing moments. A space where making mistakes is part of the process, not something to fear.

🌍

Open to everyone

Every background, every belief, every nationality. Our core value is unity in diversity — conversations are richer when everyone brings a different perspective.

💬

Conversations that matter

We explore topics like virtues, resilience, community, and purpose. Not just grammar — real things people actually want to talk about.

🌱

More than English

Members visit aged care homes, organise beach cleanups, and support each other through the challenges of life in Australia. Language is the door. Community is the destination.
